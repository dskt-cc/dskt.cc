## Desktop Mate Modding Community

The Desktop Mate modding community is a vibrant group of developers and enthusiasts working together to enhance the Desktop Mate experience. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced modder, there are several ways to get involved and find help.

Join our dedicated modding community on Discord:

- Direct help from experienced modders
- Share your work-in-progress mods

- Get early feedback on your mods
